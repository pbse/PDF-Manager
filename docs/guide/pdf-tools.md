# Core PDF Tools

Pinnacle includes a suite of high-performance PDF manipulation tools powered by Rust. These tools are designed to be fast, reliable, and secure.

## ➕ Merge & Split

Managing document volume is easy with our organization tools:

- **Merge PDFs:** Select multiple files from your library or local disk to combine them into a single PDF.
- **Split PDF:** Extract specific pages or ranges from a large document into new, smaller files.
- **Page Reordering:** Drag and drop pages to reorganize your document structure before saving.

## 🖊️ Signing & Security

Professional document management requires trust and security:

- **Digital Signatures:** Apply secure digital signatures to your documents.
- **Watermarking:** Add "Draft", "Confidential", or custom image watermarks to protect your intellectual property.
- **Sanitization:** Remove hidden metadata and sensitive information from files before sharing.
- **Forensic Redaction:** Permanently "burn in" redactions so they can never be reversed by viewers.

## 📝 Annotation & Markup

Pinnacle's annotation engine is built for deep research:
- **Highlighters:** Color-code your findings across different documents.
- **Comments:** Add sticky notes and threaded comments directly on the page.
- **Freehand Drawing:** Use pen tools for quick sketches or margin notes.
- **Extraction:** Quickly copy text or images from the viewer into your research notepad.

## 🔄 Version Control

Never lose track of your edits:
- **Version Tracking:** Pinnacle automatically tracks changes when you save edits to a document.
- **Side-by-Side Comparison:** Open two versions of the same document to visually see exactly what has changed between them.
